1995–1998 Education and Early Software Development
1995 – 1996
  • Studied Information Systems (Business Informatics) at the University of Münster, Germany.
  • Developed early software solutions for small businesses.
  • Focused on databases, client-server architectures, and enterprise applications.
1997
  • Worked as a research assistant at the University of Münster.
  • Participated in projects involving production planning and simulation systems.
  • Deepened expertise in object-oriented software engineering.
1998
  • Completed a diploma thesis on internet-based learning systems.
  • Created the foundation for InterUSS (Internet-based University Support System), which later evolved into OpenUSS.
  • Began working extensively with Java during the early J2EE era.
1999–2003 OpenUSS, Open Source, and Java Pioneer
1999

  • Expanded the InterUSS project.
  • Started publishing extensively on Java, XML, databases, and enterprise software.
2000

  • Promoted open-source technologies in academia and enterprise environments.

Enterprise Architect & Technology Leader
